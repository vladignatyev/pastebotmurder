Introduction

ShotBuf consist from two parts: desktop client and mobile client. Desktop client is the phantom application which grabs your pastes and pull them into Dropbox shared folder, so mobile clients could synchronize and get new update content from the folder.

To use ShotBuf you are required to have an Dropbox account. If you're not familiar with Dropbox let us say that: Dropbox is a home for all your photos, docs, videos, and files. Anything you add to Dropbox will automatically show up on all your computers, phones and even the Dropbox website â€” so you can access your stuff from anywhere. (See Dropbox tour to know more).

So you should create Dropbox account, if you haven't done it already.
