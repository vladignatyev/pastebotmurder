Extremely easy way
to share and to send content

To any place. From any device

  For Mac and PC      


Once ShotBuf installed clipboard of your desktop will be safely synced via Dropbox against all of your devices each time you copy and paste things using Command-C (Ctrl-C for PC). Links, images, screenshots and text notes are supported!

You're not required to know something new. Just copy your content on desktop and it will automatically appear on devices and in your Dropbox.

Features

  • – OS X (10.8 and later), Windows and iOS platforms friendly
  • – Supports iOS 6 and 7
  • – Uses your Dropbox account
  • – Works over Wi-Fi, 3G, LTE
  • – Saves text notes, links, e-mails and images
  • – Supports all of your beloved software
  • – Images preview, that has long been a dream of the mobile UI designers
  • – It syncs your clipboard, so it works in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Inkscape, GIMP and possibly any image and text editor and doesn't need any plugins
  • – No subscriptions. No limits
  • – For housewifes and for professionals
